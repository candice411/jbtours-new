DAY TRIPS by TRAIN:

There are only two companies in Gauteng hosting day trips by train. This is ideal for families, companies, churches, clubs, etc. and especially for children who have never been on a train trip. Both these companies haul the train with a steam locomotive engine!

Pretoria area: Friends of the Rail Contact Mr. Arno Victor at Tel: 082 293 4616 or sales@friendsoftherail.com or visit their website at http://www.friendsoftherail.com. They travel mostly from Pretoria to Cullinan & Bela Bela.

KZN (Durban & PMB) area: Umgeni Steam Rail Contact Mr. Rob McGregor at Tel: 031 303 3003 or robbie@umgenisteamrailway.co.za or visit their website at http://www.umgenisteamrailway.co.za/. They travel mostly from Inchanga & Kloof.

Cape Town area: Atlantic Rail Contact them at Tel: (021) 556-1012 or info@atlanticrail.co.za or visit their website at http://www.atlanticrail.co.za         They travel mostly from Cape Town station to close by destinations in the Peninsula and Boland.
