CONSUMER PROTECTION ACT

The Consumer Protection Act (CPA) has been affected from 31st March 2011 and it affects just about everyone in South Africa. We at JB Train Tours have sought legal advice on the Act and have updated all our relevant documents and procedures accordingly. However, we all still need to learn & understand by experience the affect of it on us all.

One of the stipulations is that we are not allowed to send you unwanted information. According to our records, you have subscribed or have requested to be subscribed to receive this monthly newsletter. If you do NOT want to receive it anymore, please unsubscribe at the bottom of this e-mail and you’ll be unsubscribed from our database automatically. However, we would be sad to ‘see you go’!

We are looking forward in availing relevant info on Rail Tourism in South Africa to you on a regular basis.

Cape Town stylish weekend TRAIN TOUR – every weekend of the year!

JB Train Tours hosts a Cape Town Stylish train tour every weekend of the year. This tour is very popular among honeymooners, people who want to get away for a weekend, couples who celebrate their anniversaries, corporate clients, team building companies and families.

Included in this four day package that departs on a Thursday afternoon is a one way trip from Jo’burg to Cape Town on the upmarket Premier Classe train (5-course meals & high teas and the use of bedding & towels on the train), 2 nights B&B in either the 4-star Fountains Hotel or Strand Towers Hotel in Cape Town, transfer to Cape Town airport and the flight back to Jo’burg.

2011 Vodacom Durban July Stylish weekend – from JHB

Unrivalled as Africa's greatest sporting and social event, 2 July 2011 will again attract more than 50 000 of the country's bold, brash and beautiful to Greyville Racecourse in Durban for the R3 million Vodacom Durban July.

With 12 races on the day and the best horses in the country competing in the various events, it provides a banquet of exhilarating equine competition unrivalled in Africa. The kaleidoscope of colourful and eye-stopping fashion, beautiful women and thousands of revellers enjoying themselves in the marquees and picnic sites is enhanced by the growing sense of anticipation to create an electrifying atmosphere as they line the course and the gladiators step out on parade.

The JB Train Tours train tour package on the Premier Classe train is from Fri 1 - Mon 4 July 2011 at a cost of only R4 260 pp sharing.

Contact Jolene at jolene@jbtours.co.za

SHOWS at the BARNYARD THEATRE in BOKSBURG
19 April to 29 May 2011

Super Troupers
A Tribute to ABBA, Bee Gees and the 70’s

What do ABBA and Bee Gees have in common? Both groups ruled the world in the 70’s with their monumental music and have become best selling artists of all time, selling more than 200 million records each!

The Barnyard Theatre Productions introduces a tribute to two of the most influential pop groups of our time, namely the Swedish pop sensation ABBA and the singing trio of brothers known as Bee Gees.

Beyond the tight pants, blow-dried hair and sequins, it is the catchy song hooks, simple lyrics and beautiful melodies of the superbly crafted songs which continue to endure more than 30 years later.

Super Troupers features an awesome 10 piece cast that covers all the hits of the two super groups starting with the early days of Bee Gees in the late sixties with harmonic soft rock hits such as Words, I’ve Gotta Get a Message to You, Massachusetts, New York Mining Disasterand many more. 

Tel: (011) 823-6933 or http://www.barnyardtheatre.co.za
