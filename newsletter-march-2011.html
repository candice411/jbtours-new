SHOWS at the BARNYARD THEATRE in BOKSBURG
19 April to 29 May 2011

Super Troupers
A Tribute to ABBA, Bee Gees and the 70’s

What do ABBA and Bee Gees have in common? Both groups ruled the world in the 70’s with their monumental music and have become best selling artists of all time, selling more than 200 million records each!

The Barnyard Theatre Productions introduces a tribute to two of the most influential pop groups of our time, namely the Swedish pop sensation ABBA and the singing trio of brothers known as Bee Gees.

Beyond the tight pants, blow-dried hair and sequins, it is the catchy song hooks, simple lyrics and beautiful melodies of the superbly crafted songs which continue to endure more than 30 years later.

Super Troupers features an awesome 10 piece cast that covers all the hits of the two super groups starting with the early days of Bee Gees in the late sixties with harmonic soft rock hits such as Words, I’ve Gotta Get a Message to You, Massachusetts, New York Mining Disasterand many more. 

Tel: (011) 823-6933 or http://www.barnyardtheatre.co.za

DAY TRAIN TRIPS

There are only two companies in Gauteng & one in KZN hosting day trips by train. This is ideal for families, companies, churches, clubs, etc. and especially for children who have never been on a train trip. Both these companies haul the train with a steam locomotive engine!

Pretoria area:             Friends of the Rail     Contact Mr. Arno Victor at Tel: 082 293 4616 or sales@friendsoftherail.com or visit their website at http://www.friendsoftherail.com                     They travel mostly from Pretoria to Cullinan & Bela Bela..

Johannesburg area:   Reef Steamers           Contact Mr. Les Smith at Tel: 011 478 7787 or bookings@reefsteamers.co.za or visit their website at http://www.reefsteamers.co.za     They travel mostly from Maraisburg to Magaliesburg.

KZN (Durban & PMB) area:            Umgeni Steam Rail                Contact Mr. Rob McGregor at Tel: 031-303 3003; 082-353 6003 or 087-808 7715 or robbie@umgenisteamrailway.co.za or visit their website at http://www.umgenisteamrailway.co.za/. They travel mostly from Inchanga, Kloof & PMB.

Cape Town area:            Atlantic Rail                Contact them at Tel: (021) 556-1012 or info@atlanticrail.co.za or visit their website at http://www.atlanticrail.co.za         They travel mostly from Cape Town station to close by destinations in the Peninsula and Boland.
