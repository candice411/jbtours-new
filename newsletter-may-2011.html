NAMAQUALAND FLOWERS

In August and September every year, the flowers of Namaqualand are in their splendid beauty. We have been hosting train tours from Jo’burg and Durban to this flower spectacle for more than a decade and every year people say “I should have done this many years ago”. During 2011 we’ll be hosting again Tourist Class train tours from Durban More info and from Johannesburg More info Three upmarket Premier Classe train tours will be running from Jo’burg to Namaqualand in 2011 as well More info

The recent early snow in the Western Cape and lots of rain in Namaqualand will bolster the flowers and it promises to be a GOOD flower season.

NAMIBIAN TRAIN TOURS

We’ll be hosting again two Namibian train tours during 2011, taking place in August and September of this year, departing from Jo’burg. Both tours are 10 days long and visit popular tourist destinations like: Fish River Canyon, Windhoek, Etosha, Swakopmund, Spitzkoppe, Giant’s Playground, Quiver Tree Forest, a wine estate(!) and the Namib Desert. One sleeps every night on the heritage train and two meals per day are included in the tour package. En suite 2-bed compartments are available (and VERY popular), whilst daily excursions are undertaken by buses. Experiences tour guides travel along. More info

GAUTRAIN for OUR PASSENGERS

The Gautrain in Gauteng will be fully operational as from 1st July 2011. It will run between Hatfield (in Pretoria) and Pretoria’s main railway station via Centurion, Midrand, Marlboro, Sandton and Rosebank to Johannesburg Park railway station from where our train tours depart. Passengers on the East Rand can board the Gautrain at Rhodesfield (Kempton Park) or at OR Tambo Airport to travel to JHB Park station.

A one-way ticket from Pretoria to Jo’burg Park station is currently only R46 per person, whilst the journey will take you only 35 minutes! Our opinion is that many of our Pretoria and Centurion based passengers will make use of the Gautrain to join our train tours in Jo’burg. Ample safe long term parking is available at PTA’s station.

2011 Vodacom Durban July Stylish weekend – from JHB

Unrivalled as Africa's greatest sporting and social event, 2 July 2011 will again attract more than 50 000 of the country's bold, brash and beautiful to Greyville Racecourse in Durban for the R3 million Vodacom Durban July.

With 12 races on the day and the best horses in the country competing in the various events, it provides a banquet of exhilarating equine competition unrivalled in Africa. The kaleidoscope of colourful and eye-stopping fashion, beautiful women and thousands of revellers enjoying themselves in the marquees and picnic sites is enhanced by the growing sense of anticipation to create an electrifying atmosphere as they line the course and the gladiators step out on parade.

The JB Train Tours train tour package on the Premier Classe train is from Fri 1 - Mon 4 July 2011 at a cost of only R4 260 pp sharing. Contact Jolene at jolene@jbtours.co.za
