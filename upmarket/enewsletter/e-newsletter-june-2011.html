MAURITIUS PRESIDENT AGAIN

One of our tour guides, Mauritius Meiring, has been (re)elected as the National President of the Ulysses Motorbike Club of South Africa. After serving for six consecutive years as president, he stood down at the last AGM to make place for ‘new blood’. Unfortunately the ‘new blood’ has vacated office within a few months and at the May 2011 AGM Mauritius was requested (‘Forced’) to take the hot seat again.

Ulysses MC is by far the biggest motorbike club in SA with more than 1500 members country wide.

The slogan of this club: "Growing old disgracefully!"

Mauritius is a well know personality in the biking fraternity in South Africa and he leads our Motorbike-on-Train tours to the Western and Eastern Cape as well.

MORE Jo’burg to Cape Town TRAIN TOURS

Our Jo’burg to Cape Town train tours are growing by leaps and bounds. The popularity of these tours is BIG. It seems that South Africans living in the ‘Northern’ areas of our country have decided that they HAVE TO VISIT the Mother City at least once in their lifetime.
