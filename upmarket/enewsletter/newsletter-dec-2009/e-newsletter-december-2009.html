FEEDBACK on TOURS – November 2009
7 Provinces & 3 Trains – Circular route through South Africa     Boarding in Jo’burg (or en route) in the evening, breakfast in Durban, lunch on the train (near Bloemfontein) the next day, breakfast in Cape Town the following day, dinner at Beaufort West (on the train) the next day and another two nights in Gauteng – it sounds like a ‘jet setter’s life’ but it is part and parcel of this 7-day round-robin tour through South Africa. We had passenger pick-up points at 12 different cities and towns on this tour.

Cherry Festival – Pretoria & Jo’burg to Clarens & Ficksburg   This luxury bus tour visited the Cherry Festival, Clarens, Fouriesburg, Golden Gate and beautiful mountainous Eastern Free State. The new hotel at Clarens (Mont D’Or) was impressive, as was the country side and places visited.

Vic Falls to Jo’burg via Zambia, Zimbabwe, Botswana, Mozambique & Swaziland – Visitors from abroad, as well as a number of South Africans, enjoyed this 15-day tour, visiting the neighbouring countries, including Vic Falls and Chobe Game Park.


Vic Falls from the Zambian side

Durban stylish weekend – Jo’burg to Durban – A group of friends traveled to the holiday city to enjoy a good weekend in town.

Cape Town weekend – Jo’burg to Stellenbosch – A well known company had their centenary celebrations on the train as well as in Stellenbosch. They’ve booked the wholePremier Classe train for the trip (84 passengers), slept one night in a hotel outside Stellenbosch & flew back: Thursday to Saturday.

Cape Town stylish weekend – Jo’burg to Cape Town – Passengers traveled on the Premier Classe, slept two nights in a hotel in CPT & flew back to Jo’burg on the Sunday afternoon. The late “winter” rains surprised everyone!
