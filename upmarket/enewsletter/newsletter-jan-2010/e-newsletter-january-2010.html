THIS was 2009 DANKIE aan 2009
The year of 2009 was a major challenge to many people, businesses, corporate companies, industries and institutions. The recession has hit hard at all levels and at all places. The tourism industry world wide was negatively affected as well. For many it was ‘a bridge too far’ in financial terms. We trust that everyone that has experienced a negative and tough 2009 will have a GOOD one in 2010.

Our company, JB Train Tours, has experienced our best year ever! We had growth in all our departments. The number of passengers per year that travelled with us increased, the number of tours hosted increased, new destinations were added, the number of regular passengers grew and the novices grew as well!

Why this good fortune on us? There could be many reasons, but we’ll highlight only a few:

Our clients stayed loyal to us
We offered an affordable, comfortable and safe product
Our staff are passionate about what they do
We increased our advertising and promotion campaigns
South Africans realised that touring IN South Africa could be fun and value for money as well – especially by train tour
The train tours brought back sweet memories and nostalgy of gone-by years
The traveling needs of many people were addressed
Shosholoza Meyl improved their services to us
AND probably the best reason:
We were blessed from Above.

Baie dankie aan elkeen van u wat hierdie nuusbrief lees. Ons doel is om aan te hou groei en meer & lekker treintoere aan u te bied. Kom reis (weer) saam met ons.

WORLD CUP SOCCER
This is our BIG year. Our = South Africa. Our country will be the host nation for one of the biggest events (not sport only) ever to be hosted anywhere in the world. The official dates of the FIFA World Cup is from 11th June to the 11th July 2010, but long before and after those dates, spectators, supporters, dignataries, members of the press and TV crews, heads of states and many more will be flocking to our country.

If you are not involved yet – do get involved. Just buy one ticket to a match and go and enjoy it. Even if you don’t know the game nor like it. Go for the ‘happening’. You can purchase tickets at any FNB Bank or on the internet. Link

BAVIAANSKLOOF – ELKE ‘BIKER’ se DROOM
Die Baviaanskloof-roete in die Oos Kaap (tussen Patensie en Willowmore) is vir die meeste 4x4 & ‘off road’ motorfiets entoesiaste ‘n droom & roete wat gery MOET word. Besoek http://www.baviaanskloof.za.cx/ en http://www.baviaans.co.za/ om te sien hoe dit daar lyk. Die skrywer van hierdie nuusbrief het in Desember 2009 sy motorfiets op die trein in Johannesburg gelaai, oornag na Beaufort Wes gery, deur Meiringspoort na Oudtshoorn gery (vir sake), toe na George en die Wildernis en toe sommer ook die Baviaanskloof saam met sy neef, Du Toit Viljoen van Durbanville, op hul motorfietse deurgery.

Oppad terug het hy ook die bekende (en baie mooi) R62 roete gery en om die baie motorfietsryery op ‘n (letterlik) hoë noot af te sluit oor die bekende en baie mooi Swartbergpas na Prins Albert gery om by Beaufort Wes weer met die motorfiets op die trein te klim – terug na JHB. (Hy het ook “Die Hel” (Gamkaskloof) besoek).

Wat kan ons verder sê: Die droom het waar geword en die rit was beter as die droom! Altemit moet ons maar ‘n Motorfiets-op-Treintoer vir ‘dual purpose bikes’ soontoe reël…
