NEW Jo’burg to Durban TRAIN TOUR

Durban remains a popular holiday destination for many people from ‘up country’ and we have put (again) together a Budget weekend train tour from Jo’burg to the Holiday City. This new tour will travel only on the Trans Natal Tourist Class train and departs on a Thursday evening at 18:20 from Jo’burg to arrive the next morning in Durban. Two days are spent in Durban (including one night in an upmarket hotel, B&B) before returning back to Jo’burg to arrive the Sunday morning home. Meals and transport in Durban are included in the tour price of R1650 pp. This tour promises to be very popular. Only two tours are scheduled for 2010: 23 to 26 Sept & 16 to 19 Dec 2010. Both tours are during the school holiday.

The Durban beachfront has been upgraded during 2010 when more than R200 million was spent on amenities. Our hotel is right opposite the upgraded area.

NAMAQUALAND DAISIES - 2010

Sea at Yzerfontein

We have just (first week in June 2010) returned from our annual recce (visiting all the service providers) in Namaqualand and the West Coast of South Africa and had the opportunity to view the first flowers of the season. It promises to be a good season and the local folk are awaiting our visits.


Zebra in the mist - Langebaan

We’ll be hosting 20 train tours from Johannesburg and Durban respectively to the Namaqualand Flowers this year during August and September. The inaugural Premier Classe daisy train will be running, whilst we’ll be hosting the inaugural Motorbike-on-Train tour to the flowers as well. The latter is hosted on dual purpose bikes. I.e.: Riding on tar and gravel roads.

If you have never been to the daisies, go this year. A visit to that region is a visit to more than just a visit to view the flowers. It is to live and experience the people, food, friendliness and places off the region.

HET U GEWEET?

Cape Town’s new ‘Big wheel’

- Dat Kaapstad nou ook ‘n ‘Groot Wiel’ het. Dis by die ingang na die V&A Waterfront, naby die Kaapstadse Konvensiesentrum, geleë. Wanneer u derhalwe saam met een van ons Kaapstad treintoere reis, gaan maak ‘n draai daar. Dit is binne loopafstand vanaf ons hotel en bied ‘n pragtige uitsig oor die stad.

- Meer inligting rondom die ‘Big Wheel’: Dit is 50 meter in deursnee (dieselfde hoogte as ‘n 15 verdieping gebou), dit het 36 kapsules waarin 8 mense elk pas, dit duur sowat 20 minute om ‘n volle omwenteling te doen en kos R70 per volwassene.

- Dat Kurt Darren se tweede inniBosveld musiekfees op Vrydag 18 en Saterdag 19 Februarie 2011 op sy bosveldplaas naby Naboomspruit in Limpopo plaasvind. Ons maatskappy is weer versoek om die bustoere soontoe te reël. Meer volledige inligting volg binnekort.
