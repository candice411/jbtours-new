CAPE TOWN JAZZ FESTIVAL

The 11th annual Cape Town Jazz Festival will be hosted again over the coming Easter weekend in Cape Town. More than 40 local and international artists will be performing in the Cape Town International Conventions Centre. In 2009 more than 32000 people attended.

JB Train Tours have organised a train tour to this event on short notice with (unfortunately) limited space available. The tour departs on Easter Friday, 2nd April 2010 from Jo’burg and will arrive back on Tuesday, 6th April 2010. Accommodation is in Cape Town in a top hotel across the street from the Jazz Festival, meals, as well as the Entry Passto the two-day Jazz festival are included in the tour package. Tour price is R3600 pp sharing. More info
 
inniBOSVELD MUSIEKFEES

Die eerste inniBosveld musiekfees het op Saterdag 20 Februarie 2010 op die sanger Kurt Darren, se bosveldplaas buite Naboomspruit plaasgevind. BUFFALO RALLY TRAIN TOUR

The first Buffalo Rally for bikers (visit www.nomads.org.za) was held in 1969 at Bathurst in the Eastern Cape. +-250 Bikers attended. In 2010 the ‘Buff’ will be hosted again at Santos/De Bakke municipal resorts in Mossel Bay – this time many 1000’s of bikers will attend.

To make life easier for bikers from ‘up country’, JB Train Tours has organised a train trip for bikers (with their bikes and pillions with them on the train) from Jo’burg to Beaufort West. After off loading they’ll ride the 253 km to Mossel Bay via spectacular Meiringspoort & over the Robinson Pass.

The tour is from Wednesday 17th March to Monday 22nd March 2010 (Monday is a Public Holiday) and the tour price is R3100 pp. The pillion pays less. More info
