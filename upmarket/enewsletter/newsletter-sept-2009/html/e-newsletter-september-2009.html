Namaqualand Flowers – Durban to Namaqualand – “When traveling from the green pastures of KZN, through the dry Free State and Karoo, the beauty of the Western Cape and Namaqualand is grace for the eyes” – Verdict of one of the passengers on tour!

Rugby Test – Jo’burg to Durban by Premier Classe train. (‘Bok train)– It was good to experience the Springboks beating the All Blacks on two successive Saturdays. To be present at a test match is always something special.

Rugby Test – JHB to CPT by Premier Classe train. (‘Bok train) – The Springboks have beaten the Wallabies in the Tri Nations test at Newlands in Cape Town as well. Hence it was another good tour and another succesful ‘Bok train’!

Namibia– Windhoek to Jo’burg – Tour # 4 has just departed from the capital of Namibia to explore the beauty of our neighbouring country. On ‘going to press’ the tour group was in the Etosha Game Reserve.
