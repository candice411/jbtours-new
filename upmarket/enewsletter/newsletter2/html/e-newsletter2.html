FOUR ROUTES
The Premier Classe train currently runs on four routes through South Africa
   
1. Johannesburg to Cape Town and Cape Town to Johannesburg
2. Johannesburg to Durban and Durban to Johannesburg
3. Cape Town to Port Elizabeth and Port Elizabeth to Cape Town (both legs via Oudtshoorn)
4. Cape Town to Oudtshoorn and Cape Town to Oudtshoorn return

CAR TRANSPORT
Why don’t you take your car with you on the next trip?

 
Car transport carriage
 
It is possible to transport your vehicle (car/4x4/motorbike) with you on the train. The price varies according to the size of the car and of the relevant season. This service is currently only available on the following routes:
 
1. Johannesburg to Cape Town and Cape Town to Johannesburg
2. Johannesburg to Durban and Durban to Johannesburg
 
This service is not available on the Cape Town to Port Elizabeth and Port Elizabeth to Cape Town route but will be introduced soon – place yourself on our waiting list.
 
Take your car with on your next trip to Cape Town so that you will be able to travel in the comfort of your own car!

NAMIBIA

The only train tours to Namibia in 2009 are hosted by JB Train Tours
 



If you travel with your own car to Namibia and you travel the same distances as we do with our train and buses on tour, it will costs you R3.28 per km (excluding accommodation, meals, bus transport, cross border fees, sight seeing and tour guides). Calculations were done on current fuel prices.

If you travel with our train tour to Namibia and you travel the same distances as you would do with your own car, it will costs you only R2.51 per km (including 9 nights accommodation, two meals per day, bus transport excursions, sight seeing and tour guides)

Conclusion: it is much more affordable to travel by train tour to Namibia PLUS you don’t have the hassle of doing the planning of your trip, neither doing the bookings, getting lost, missing out on popular and beautiful tourist spots.
