JB Train Tours (Since 1975) has launched its Upmarket Train Tour Packages on the luxurious Premier Classe train on Friday 14th November 2008.

We have been hosting train tours for the budget market for many years to various destinations in Southern Africa, but has identified the need to host shorter train tours for corporate companies, groups, clubs and individuals in the upmarket echelons – but still at very affordable and competitive prices.

  
   

Our upmarket train tour packages include the overnight train trip on the luxurious Premier Classe train between Johannesburg, Durban and Cape Town, 5-course fine cuisine meals, bus transport to and from the station at destination, a JB Train Tours representative and accommodation off the train when applicable.  We have various optional services.
