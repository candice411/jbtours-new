ABOUT CAPE TOWN AND EN ROUTE
  

This 4-day tour package is a great way of spending your honeymoon, celebrate a very special occasion or just get away for a mini-break

Spend some time in one of the MUST SEE places in South Africa: Cape Town – the Mother City of South Africa. (Cape Town was listed Number 2 of “Places to visit before you die” in an International survey).

This is where Robben Island (where Nelson Mandela spent 27 years of his life) is situated, fabulous Table Mountain, spectacular Cape Point, the V&A Waterfront, the wine routes of the Cape (including Great Constantia), Hout Bay and Kirstenbosch Botanical Gardens (with its variety of flowers).

There are many ways of travelling from Gauteng to Cape Town, but travelling with JB Train Tours on the infamous Blue train will give you the opportunity to see so much more of South Africa. You will travel through 4 of the 9 provinces of South Africa (Gauteng, North West, Northern Cape & Western Cape); you’ll experience the vastness of the Karoo and the beautiful sunsets; you’ll see Springbuck and some other antelope en route and you’ll enjoy the beauty of the vineyards of the Western Cape.

Various popular tourist destinations are within close range of the hotel. I.e.: “Capart Theatre” & Long Street Restaurants (romantic dinners!) and night clubs are within walking distance, whilst the V & A Waterfront is a brisk shuttle by bus or taxi away.
